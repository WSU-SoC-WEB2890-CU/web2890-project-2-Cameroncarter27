Events

At UEI, we are dedicated to empowering communities and tackling pressing issues facing Utahns, such as the rising student lunch debt crisis. On June 8th, 2024, UEI joined forces with the community of Ogden for the “Rockin’ Ogden Benefit Concert,” an event held at the Dee Event Center. This gathering not only aimed to raise funds but also to raise awareness about the staggering $2.8 million student lunch debt in Utah, which escalated by $870,000 in 2024 alone.

The concert featured exhilarating performances by local favorites “Late Night Savior” and the acclaimed band Hinder, whose electrifying sets resonated with attendees, fostering a spirit of unity and action. Beyond music, the event offered food vendors, interactive activities, and informational booths that shed light on the profound impact of lunch debt on students and their families.

The proceeds from the “Rockin’ Ogden Benefit Concert” directly benefited schools across Utah, providing crucial support to alleviate student lunch debt and ensure that every child has access to nutritious meals. At UEI, we believe in leveraging such collective efforts to drive systemic change while advocating for legislative reforms that sustain positive impacts. Through events like these and ongoing community engagement, UEI continues to empower Utahns to address challenges proactively and build a brighter future for all.
