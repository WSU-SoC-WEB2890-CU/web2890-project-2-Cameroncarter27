About Us

At UEI, we believe in fostering growth and adaptation for Utahns amidst evolving challenges. Today’s youth are tomorrow’s innovators, and they deserve an educational environment free from the stress of basic needs insecurity. With historic cost-of-living increases and worsening education outcomes, it is clear our public school system has fallen behind, impacting our children’s future.

We advocate for an integrated approach that combines creative strategies, a curious mindset, and the transformative potential of collective action. UEI empowers communities to address current issues autonomously while advocating for legislative reforms that institutionalize positive change. By harnessing collective action and driving legislative reform, we are committed to building a brighter future for all.
