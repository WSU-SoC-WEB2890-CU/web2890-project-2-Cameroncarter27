Uniting Utah’s Youth, Small Businesses, and Legislators for a Brighter Future

About Us

Welcome to the Utah Empowerment Initiative, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to fostering collaboration among Utah’s youth, small business sector, and legislators. Our mission is to build a more united Utah where homelessness is solved, hunger is eliminated, and civic engagement thrives. Join us in shaping a brighter tomorrow for all.

Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteers are the backbone of our organization. We offer opportunities for individuals and groups to get involved and make a difference in the local community and beyond! Check out our events page to see what we’re up to!
