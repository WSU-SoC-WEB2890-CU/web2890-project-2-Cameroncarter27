School Lunch Debt

41 School Districts

Spanning over 29 counties, many schools report difficulty covering student lunch debt balances at the end of the year, forcing them to divert funds from education and recreational programs. Further disadvantaging at risk youth.

660,000 Students

Over 435,000 students are not currently covered with reduced or free lunch due to lack of funding, and Title 1 benefits being stretched thin.

$2.8 million

An increase of $870,000 in student lunch debt from 2023, a clear indicator that current strategies are insufficient.
